BURGER RECIPE

Ingredient
Quantity
Ground Beef 4 lb
Cracked Pepper 2 tsp
Kosher Salt 2 tsp
Dijon Mustard 4 tbsp
Worcestershire Sauce 3 tbs
Egg 1
Ham 2

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until well blended.

  2. Divide mixture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide.

  3. Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over a solid bed of hot coals or high heat on a gas grill (you can hold your hand at grill level only 2 to 3 seconds) close lid on gas grill.

  4. Cook burgers, turning once, until browned on both sides and no longer pink inside (cut to test), 7 to 8 minutes total. Remove from grill.

  5. Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.

  6. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms. Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place.

  7. Enjoy your Classic Burger
